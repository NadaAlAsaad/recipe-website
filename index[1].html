Banana Milkshake

Ingredients:

1-Bananas:
2 ripe bananas
2-Milk:
2 cups (can be regular, almond, or any milk of your choice)
3-Vanilla Ice Cream:
2 scoops (optional, for a creamier texture)
4-Honey or Sugar:
1 to 2 tablespoons (optional, depending on sweetness preference)
5-Vanilla Extract:
1/2 teaspoon (optional, for added flavor)
6-Ice Cubes:
3 to 4 (optional, for a chilled shake)

You can also customize with extras like peanut butter, cocoa powder, or cinnamon!

Method:

  1. Peel and Slice Bananas – Peel 2 ripe bananas and slice them into small pieces for easier blending.
  2. Add Ingredients to Blender – Place the banana slices into a blender. – Add 2 cups of milk (regular or your choice). – If using, add 2 scoops of vanilla ice cream and 1-2 tablespoons of honey or sugar. – Add 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract for extra flavor.
  3. Blend Until Smooth – Blend on high speed for 30-60 seconds until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  4. Add Ice (Optional) – If you want a chilled shake, add 3-4 ice cubes and blend again for a few seconds.
  5. Serve – Pour the milkshake into glasses and enjoy immediately!

You can also garnish it with whipped cream, a sprinkle of cinnamon, or a drizzle of chocolate syrup for an extra treat.
